FAQ

Food storage questions

Answers for food distributors, importers, FMCG brands, and retail supply teams.

Can you support dry and cold food storage?

Yes. Dry, chilled, and frozen requirements can be captured so the right storage profile is reviewed.

Do you support FEFO for expiry-date products?

FEFO or FIFO handling should be shared during quote planning so date-sensitive products are managed correctly.

Which city is best for food distribution?

Riyadh supports central distribution, Jeddah supports western routes, and Dammam supports eastern movement.

What should I include in my request?

Send product type, volume, pallet count, temperature needs, preferred city, and target start date.
