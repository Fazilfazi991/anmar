Location Planning

How we recommend a warehouse city

The best location depends on product type, inbound route, outbound demand, storage condition, and how quickly inventory needs to move.

  • Riyadh is often best for central market access and national distribution.
  • Jeddah is useful for western region inventory and port-linked supply chains.
  • Dammam supports eastern industry, Gulf movement, and industrial customers.
  • Madinah can support flexible western coverage and secondary storage needs.
